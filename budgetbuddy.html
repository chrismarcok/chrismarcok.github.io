About

BudgetBuddy designed and developed in Android Studio by Chris Marcok in August 2018 using the Java language. Uses SQLite to add, update & remove entries and tags from databases stored on your phone.

BudgetBuddy does not store any information outside of your phone. You can reset the app using the "clear data" button in the individual app's storage section.

This app has been tested using: Nexus 4, Nexus 5, OnePlus 5, Galaxy S7 Edge, Galaxy S8, Pixel, Pixel 2 XL.
